<h3><strong>The Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP)</strong></h3>
<p>The Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP) by Health Promotion Board&nbsp;(HPB)
encourages healthier food and beverage choices in schools. This&nbsp;programme
aims to help students cultivate healthy eating habits right from their
youth.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Under this programme, canteen vendors need to follow food service&nbsp;guidelines
as follows:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Cutting down fat, sugar and salt</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serving whole grains, fruit and vegetables</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serving healthy set meals which incorporate food from the 4 main food
groups- brown rice, wholemeal bread, meat and others, vegetable and fruit</p>
</li>
</ul>
