<p>As we begin our centennial journey in 2026, the <strong>TKPS100 Human Formation</strong> stands
as a powerful symbol of who we are and what we share. By coming together
as one — P1 to P6 students, staff and stakeholders — we honour the legacy
of Telok Kurau Primary School while stepping confidently into our next
century. This formation is more than a visual display; it is a collective
declaration of unity, belonging and pride. Each individual represents a
vital part of our story and together, we create something far greater than
ourselves. Captured in a time-lapse video, this special moment reflects
not just a formation but a community moving as one — a meaningful tribute
that will be preserved as part of our 100-year legacy.</p>
