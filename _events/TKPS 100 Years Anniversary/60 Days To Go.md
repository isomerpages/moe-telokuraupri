<p><strong>How did Richie become a familiar and much-loved part of TKPS?</strong>
</p>
<p>One key question shaped the decision, what kind of mascot could truly
represent TKPS and grow with our students through the years?</p>
<p></p>
<p>That question led to Richie. Over time, he has become more than just a
mascot, he represents the warmth, pride and spirit of the TKPS community.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As we mark TKPS100, we celebrate Richie, our school mascot and a cherished
symbol of the TKPS spirit.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Richie Bear" src="/images/richie_2010a.jpg">
</div>
<p>First conceptualised in 2010, Richie was created to represent the values
we stand for. The “RICH” in Richie reflects our core values, Respect, Responsibility,
Resilience, Integrity, Care and Harmony, values that continue to guide
our students in their journey. This bear was eventually chosen, one that
could walk alongside our students, grow with them and be part of their
everyday experiences.</p>
<p></p>

<p>In 2015, Richie grew quite literally into the larger than life presence
we know today. Standing at around 2 meters tall, he became an even more
familiar and exciting figure around school, bringing energy and joy wherever
he goes.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Richie Bear" src="/images/richie_2024.jpg">
</div>
<p>Whether in school uniform, out on an adventure or dressed to celebrate
the rich cultures of our community, Richie reflects the vibrancy and diversity
of TKPS.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As we celebrate 100 years, Richie continues to be a beloved part of TKPS.
As we look ahead, we can’t wait to see where Richie walks with us next.</p>
