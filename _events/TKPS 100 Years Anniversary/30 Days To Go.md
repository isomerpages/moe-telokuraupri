<p><strong><em>28 April 2026</em></strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As part of our <strong>TKPS100 celebrations</strong>, we are excited to
present our Telok Kurau Primary School Art exhibition.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Centered on the theme of <strong>Anchored in Roots, Charting our Route</strong>,
this exhibition reflects the belief that while our students are shaped
by their cultural roots and school experiences, they are not limited by
them. While these experiences provide a foundation, our students have the
agency to make choices, explore ideas and shape who they become.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Anchored in Roots represents what grounds them, while Charting Our Route
reflects how they move forward to shape their own paths and contribute
to the future.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/30_more_days.jpg">
</div>
<p>Take a look at how our students are bringing their ideas to life as a
prelude to the full exhibition. Through <strong>Batik Painting</strong>, <strong>Clay Relief Tiles</strong>, <strong>3D Modellin</strong>g, <strong>Cubism artworks</strong>, <strong>Air-dry Clay creations</strong>, <strong>Wire Sculptures</strong>, <strong>Ceramic Vases and Landscape art</strong>,
they explore, experiment and create in different ways.</p>
<p></p>
<p>These are just glimpses. The full stories and final pieces will be revealed
at the exhibition.</p>
<p>📅 3 to 29 June
<br>📍 Bedok National Library</p>
<p></p>
<p>Come experience Anchored in Roots, Charting Our Route. We cannot wait
for you to be part of our journey. ✨</p>
