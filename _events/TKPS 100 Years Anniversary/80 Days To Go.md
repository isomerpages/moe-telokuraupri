<p>The <strong>TKPS100 facade banner</strong> has been unveiled at Telok Kurau
Primary School, marking a key milestone as the school approaches its 100th
anniversary.</p>
<p>The banner features TKPS100 lettering formed by a collage of photographs
capturing student learning, innovation and community life, reflecting the
centennial theme, <strong>“Inspiring Innovation, Enabling Transformation”</strong>.</p>
<p>It stands as a symbol of the pride, memories and shared spirit of the
TKPS community.</p>
<p>TKPS100 – Soaring High.</p>
