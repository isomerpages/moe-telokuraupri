<p>The TKPS100 facade banner has been unveiled at Telok Kurau Primary School,
marking a key milestone as the school approaches its 100th anniversary.
<br>
</p>
<p>The banner features TKPS100 lettering formed by a collage of photographs
capturing student learning, innovation and community life, reflecting the
centennial theme, “Inspiring Innovation, Enabling Transformation”.</p>
<p></p>
<p>It stands as a symbol of the pride, memories and shared spirit of the
TKPS community.</p>
<p></p>
<p>TKPS100 – Soaring High.</p>
