<h5><strong>Talent Development</strong></h5>
<p>All students will gain exposure to coding through our Primary 1 to Primary
6 coding&nbsp;programme, which is integrated within the curriculum.</p>
<p>Students who show strong interest and talent in coding or robotics may
join the&nbsp;Robotics CCA to further grow their passion and skills. The
school also sends students&nbsp;to participate in both National and International
Competitions.</p>
