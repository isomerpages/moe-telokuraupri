<p>President Tharman was guest of honour at the school's centennial celebrations
on 28 May, where he emphasised that in an age of artificial intelligence,
schools must hold firm to their core mission of nurturing character and
people. He noted that while technology continues to reshape the world,
fundamental values of education, building confidence, fostering joy in
learning and strengthening human connections, must remain constant. The
article also highlights three TKPS Primary 6 students who were recently
presented with the Singapore Silent Heroes Student Award.</p>
