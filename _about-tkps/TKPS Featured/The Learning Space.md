<p>Three of our students - Wong Shun Hong from P6RF, Cheong Jia Ying from
P6RF and Dan Qian Xun from P5RF were featured in Zaobao Schools. They shared
how the school's unique learning spaces, including the aquaponics area,
the digestive system display at Richie Café and the Giving Tree, together
with initiatives like the plate and cutlery return programme, have made
learning fun, authentic and meaningful. The article highlighted how these
hands-on, real-world environments extend learning beyond the classroom
and spark genuine curiosity in students.</p>
