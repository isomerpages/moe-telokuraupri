<p>The 37th National Schools Chinese Calligraphy Competition was held on
1 May 2026 at the Singapore Calligraphy Centre, graced by Guest of Honour
Mr Foo Cexiang. The competition drew over 1600 participants, with 109 students
receiving awards. Our student Cai Xin Yun from P6SN won 2nd prize, a remarkable
achievement in this highly competitive national event.</p>
