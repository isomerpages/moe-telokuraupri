<p>TKPS clinched the championship at the 13th National Chinese Challenge
for the third time, competing against 162 schools and over 3000 students.
The finals were held on 25 April at Punggol Coast Mall. The article also
featured an interview with a P5 student who reflected on how proud she
was to be able to represent our school and the achievement was apt as a
gift to celebrate our school’s 100<sup>th</sup> anniversary.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
