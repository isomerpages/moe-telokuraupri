<p>This short article covers the 100th anniversary celebration of Telok Kurau
Primary School. President Tharman was the guest of honour. A musical concert
titled One Voice, One Heartbeat was held, with parents, former students
and alumni in attendance. The article also notes that the school counts
among its alumni the late Singapore Prime Minister Mr Lee Kuan Yew and
the late Malaysian Prime Minister Tun Hussein Onn.</p>
