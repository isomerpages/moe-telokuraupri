<p></p>
<p>This article covers the centennial celebrations of Telok Kurau Primary
School, held on 28 May at the school premises. President Tharman was guest
of honour at the event. The school noted that among its distinguished alumni
are the late Singapore Prime Minister Mr Lee Kuan Yew and the late Malaysian
Prime Minister Tun Hussein Onn. Principal Ms Grace Leong highlighted that
over the past century, the school has produced many generations of alumni
who have gone on to contribute significantly to various fields. The celebrations
also featured a musical concert titled One Voice, One Heartbeat.</p>
