<p>Students from the TKPS Lion Dance Troupe were interviewed by Zaobao to
share their experiences in this unique CCA. They expressed their pride
and joy in being part of a CCA that they find enriching, enjoyable and
meaningful. The troupe also reflects the school's inclusive culture, attracting
students from different ethnic backgrounds coming together to to learn
and perform, reflecting the school's vibrant and welcoming culture.</p>
