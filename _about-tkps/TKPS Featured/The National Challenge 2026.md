<p>Lianhe Zaobao reported on the upcoming finals of the 16th National Chinese
Challenge, scheduled for 25 April, with 12 primary and secondary schools
competing. The article highlighted how the competition encourages students
to strengthen their Chinese language skills and cultural knowledge, and
featured an interview with Mdm Pamela Leck Hwee Huan, Head of Department
of Mother Tongue Language. She said that qualifying for the finals marked
a significant breakthrough for our school this year, inspiring both teachers
and students. It was a testament to the students’ dedication and hard work.</p>
