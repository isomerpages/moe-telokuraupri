<h4><strong>Students’ Pledge</strong></h4>
<p>We, the students of Telok Kurau Primary School,
<br>pledge to uphold the name of the school,
<br>to be courteous&nbsp;and to respect
<br>our teachers and fellow schoolmates.
<br>
<br>We will obey the rules of the school,
<br>and will work hard to achieve our goals.
<br>Together, we will strive for excellence.</p>
<p>
<br>
</p>
